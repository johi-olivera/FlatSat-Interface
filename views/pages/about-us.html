What is LabOSat?

LabOSat stands for "Laboratory On a Satellite". It is an electronic platform to perform experiments in harsh environments such as outer space, low-pressure, low-temperature conditions, or under big doses of radiation.

LabOSat-01 is the first version of the platform, designed to harbor devices for electrical testing under extreme conditions. Inside LabOSat-01, there is a module called MeMOSat, which applies voltage or current sweeps on ReRAM devices and can be easily adapted to any other device.
